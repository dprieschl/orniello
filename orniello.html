The Maker

Archaeology, craft,
and living history.

I'm Lydia Valant - archaeologist, jeweler, and passionate reenactor based in Graz. My fascination with historical adornment began at 13 with my first metal piece; it has since grown into a dedicated practice bridging academic research and artisan craft.

My pieces are found in museum shops across Austria and worn at historical fairs throughout Europe. Every creation starts with a primary source - an archaeological find, a historical painting, a museum collection.
